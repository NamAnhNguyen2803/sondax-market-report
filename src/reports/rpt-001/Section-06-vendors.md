> **Key Takeaways**
> - **Food-tour specialists dominate day-tour OTA rankings** — top operators co 3,000-5,000+ reviews tao "review moat" kho vuot. New entrants can niche differentiation (vegan food, morning market, craft beer pairings) de canh tranh. [HIGH]

> - **3 supply gaps lon nhat**: (1) Mid-range wellness tours (demand $11.7B projected, supply gan nhu zero tren OTA), (2) Wet-season optimized tours (khong ai "own" narrative nay), (3) Indian market-adapted products (746K visitors, +49% growth, chua co tour optimize cho ho). [MEDIUM]
> - **Reseller vs. own-operated**: ~30-35% OTA listings la resellers — day la nguon goc cua nhieu negative reviews. Own-operated operators co rating cao hon 0.3-0.5 diem trung binh. Traveler dang ngay cang uu tien "operated by" transparency. [MEDIUM]
> - **OTA ≠ toan bo thi truong**: Nhieu operator manh (DMCs, B2B suppliers) khong visible tren OTA. OTA chi la 1 kenh — direct booking, B2B agent networks, va hostel referrals van chiem phan lon market, dac biet cho adventure/trekking segment. [MEDIUM]
