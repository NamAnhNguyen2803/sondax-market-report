## 7.2 Wet-Season Positioning Framework

> **Core insight**: Wet season (May-Oct) la FEATURE, khong phai BUG. Operators nao "own" narrative nay se co competitive moat vi KHONG AI DANG LAM.

### Framework: "Vietnam's Green Season" — 4 Pillars

| Pillar | Messaging | Destinations/Tours Supported | Target Markets | Evidence |
|--------|-----------|------------------------------|---------------|----------|
| **1. Green Landscapes** | "Rice terraces at their greenest", "lush valleys", "waterfall season" | Ha Giang Loop (May-Sep rice terraces), Sapa (Jun-Aug green), Pu Luong, Ninh Binh (May-Jun rice) | AU, UK, NZ (Adventure Seekers — landscape photography la motivation chinh) | S3: Ha Giang rice terrace green season = PEAK demand; Sapa Jun-Aug = fewer crowds + green |
| **2. Fewer Crowds** | "See Vietnam without the crowds", "authentic local interactions", "skip the queues" | Ha Long Bay, Hoi An Ancient Town, Mekong Delta, Cu Chi Tunnels — tat ca deu less crowded May-Oct | US, UK, CA (Culture-Hungry Explorers — "authentic" la keyword #1 trong forum posts) | S1: Persona 1 quote: "I'd rather visit in off-season — fewer tourists, greener landscapes"; S5: overcrowding = pain point #1 |
| **3. Rain-Proof Adventures** | "Weather-proof experiences", "rain or shine guarantee", "indoor alternatives ready" | Phong Nha caves (rain-proof), cooking classes (indoor), food tours (covered markets), Central Vietnam coast May-Aug (DRY season) | All markets — address wet-season hesitancy truc tiep | S3: Phong Nha = rain-proof; S2: food tours + cooking classes = immune voi wet season; S5: rain plan = differentiator |
| **4. Value Season** | "Premium experience, shoulder-season pricing", "same tour, better value" | All destinations — wet season = lower accommodation + tour prices | SG, IN, PH (Asian Weekend Escapers + Value-Max — price-sensitive segments) | S1: Asian markets budget-to-mid-range; currency advantage + lower wet-season prices = double value |

| 3 | **30-40% it tourists** | Gia accommodation, tour giam; queue ngan hon; trai nghiem authentic hon voi local communities | `[MEDIUM]` |
| 4 | **Mekong Delta scenic hon** | Nuoc lon = boat ride phong phu, floating market nao nhiet hon, fruit mua he da dang hon | `[MEDIUM]` |
| 5 | **Canyoneering/waterfall season** | Dalat canyoneering tot hon khi co nuoc — wet season = BETTER experience cho adventure category | `[MEDIUM]` |
| 6 | **Local festivals** | Hue Festival, Da Nang International Fireworks, Mid-Autumn Festival — event-based demand spikes. Cross-ref S8. | `[MEDIUM]` |
